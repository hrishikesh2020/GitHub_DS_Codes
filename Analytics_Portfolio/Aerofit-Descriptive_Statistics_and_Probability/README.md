# CASE STUDY: Aerofit Customer Profiling

**Title:** How Aerofit Can Improve Customer Targeting Using Descriptive Analytics & Probability

**Problem:** Aerofit wants to identify the ideal customer profile for different treadmill products to improve marketing and sales

**Metrics:**
* Age,
* Income,
* Fitness Level,
* Usage Frequency,
* Product Type (KP281, KP481, KP781),
* Probability Distributions

**Insights:**
* High-income and fitness-focused customers prefer premium models,
* While entry-level products attract younger and budget-conscious users; clear segmentation exists across income and usage patterns

**Product Recommendations:**
* Target premium products to high-income segments,
* Personalize marketing campaigns based on fitness levels, and
* Position entry-level models for beginners and price-sensitive customers
